Resources for Mental Health Support

1. Crisis Helplines

If you or someone you know is in immediate need of help, these helplines provide 24/7 support:

  • iCall: +91-22-66250050
  • Vandrevala Foundation Helpline: +91-9930100123
  • Snehi: +91-9811222220

2. Professional Counseling Services

These organizations provide professional mental health services and counseling:

  • Mind India: Mind India Website
  • The Mental Health Foundation: Mental Health Foundation Website
  • Therapify: Therapify Website

3. Support Groups and Networks

Joining a support group can provide emotional support and help individuals feel less isolated:

  • Ginger Minds: Ginger Minds Website
  • Manas Foundation: Manas Foundation Website
  • Peer Support Groups: Check local community centers or online forums for peer support groups.

4. Mental Health Resources and Information

For educational resources and mental health information:

  • National Institute of Mental Health and Neuro Sciences (NIMHANS): NIMHANS Website
  • Ministry of Health and Family Welfare: Ministry Website
  • Mental Health Matters: Mental Health Matters Website

5. Online Counseling and Therapy

For remote support, consider these online platforms:

  • Wysa: Wysa Website
  • BetterHelp: BetterHelp Website
  • Talkspace: Talkspace Website

For more personalized support, consider reaching out to local mental health professionals or organizations. Early intervention and support can make a significant difference in mental health management and recovery.
